Edward Frenkel is a Professor of Mathematics at University of California at Berkeley. Among his awards are the Hermann Weyl Prize, Packard Fellowship in Science and Engineering and the first Chaire d’Excellence award from Foundation Sciences Mathématiques de Paris. Editor of seven mathematical journals, he recently authored two books – Langlands Correspondence for Loop Groups – and over 80 articles in mathematical journals. Frenkel is currently completing his third book, The Langlands Program and Quantum Field Theory.

Edward Frenkel, Ph.D.
