Our story

Getting sick should not be the trigger for seeking health care. Being healthy should be.
Medical+Intelligence believes that individual health outcomes can be optimized through better medical decisions based on the best available evidence, inference and reasoning.

As a result, patients will build closer relationships with their doctors, have more involvement and control over their specific conditions, and carry out day-by-day actions to improve and extend their lives.
At no cost to the primary physician, we provide platforms that allow medical professionals to easily access relevant current and established data – as well asthe newest research findings as they are published, filtered by priority – for the diseases and disorders that most affect the lives of their patients.

And we accelerate the utilization of evidence-based diagnostic and monitoring tools for better patient outcomes using applications that can be accessed by the doctor/patient team – on the computer in your office, or just about anywhere using a smartphone or tablet. These new technologies provide information on an "as-needed" basis, encourage communication and feedback, and improve physician and patient decision-making, freeing both from the traditional barriers to getting the appropriate care and treatment.

Our mission at Medical+Intelligence is to work in partnership with doctors, providing them the technology and human resources they need to offer more specialized healthcare. This in turn creates a deeper doctor-patient relationship and, for the patient, confidence that they truly are getting a superior level of personalized medicine.

Andy Peiffer, M.D., Ph.D

Dr. Peiffer is a co-founder and heads Lineagen's pediatric genetics efforts within Lineagen’s Clinical Services Team.

A practicing physician with a current appointment in Pediatric Genetics at the University of Utah, Dr. Peiffer combines expertise in both the medical and scientific significance of clinical genetics. Dr. Peiffer earned his Bachelor’s degree in Physiology from McGill University, and his graduate/postgraduate degrees in Medicine, Pharmacology and Physiology from Laval University.

Robb Rowley, M.D

Dr. Rowley is a board-certified Internal Medicine Physician, completing his Internal Medicine residency training at Wilford Hall Medical Center after attending the University of Iowa College of Medicine.

Dr. Rowley previously served as the United States Air Force Surgeon General's Office as the Chief of Medical Bioinformatics and Genomics. Dr. Rowley has also been instrumental in establishing national and international plans and policies for biosurveillance systems and biotechnology for the DoD and NATO. Dr. Rowley is currently adjunct Associate Professor at Touro University in Las Vegas, Nevada.

David Sundwall, M.D

Dr. Sundwall is a Clinical Professor of Public Health at the University of Utah where he has been a faculty member since 1978. He served as Executive Director of the Utah Department of Health and Commissioner of Health for the State of Utah, and currently serves Chair of the Utah State Controlled Substance Advisory Committee.

Dr. Sundwall also serves as Vice Chair of the federal Medicaid and CHIP Payment and Access Commission [MACPAC] in Washington D.C. He received his medical degree from the University of Utah - School of Medicine, and completed residency in the Harvard Family Medicine Program.

Hugh V. Cochrane

Hugh Cochrane is currently CEO of Villay Asset Management, a firm specializing in global equity investment as well as venture capital with offices Vero Beach, Fla, and London, UK.

Mr. Cochrane began his investment career in 1997 focusing on global equities, and later diversified into a portfolio of private venture capital investments targeting new and innovative technologies. Mr. Cochrane serves on the board of directors for Plasticell, Ltd., a UK based stem cell technology and regenerative drug-discovery company. Prior to entering the field of equity investing, Mr. Cochrane spent a decade in the US apparel industry, where he held several positions including retail executive for Saks Fifth Avenue. Mr. Cochrane is a 1987 graduate of The University of Virginia.
